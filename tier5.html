Tier5 Outbound Sales Lead Generating Platform

The Client

Tier5, an early stage start-up, engaged us to update their outbound sales-lead generating platform (which contained only a very basic bulk-lead generator) and to give their product a visual facelift. But after doing extenisive research we found they need much more. Our redesign increased their market opportunity by 3x.

My Role

Over 2 weeks our very close-knit, two-person team, worked collaberatively with every aspect of UX Design process: research, user interviews, user testing, design, sketching, creating low and medium fidelity protoytypes, and presenting our work.
