Research & Discovery

21

Sales Representatives Surveyed

In order to understand what a sales representative needs, we created survey questions about their prospecting, qualifying, and closing process. This gave us a great initial direction on our designs and helped us to develop our more extensive interview questions.

10

Users Interviewed

The interview process gave us a more in-depth perspective about how salespeople go about nuturing clients and upselling.

Survey resulted in 4 key insights

Most salespeople are one of two main types:

High Touch, Low Volume      Low Touch, High Volume

Waste of time & money

62% of individuals use two or more platforms to find leads

Need information to "nuture" leads

76% of individuals thought it would be useful to have a newsfeed to improve the "nurturing" of a lead in order to close and up-sell

Willingness to help imporve the algorithm

95% of individuals were willing to give some feedback to improve the accuracy of Tier5's contact information

In-depth interview insights

It's hard to make a good first impression

"Approaching any lead takes lots of sweat, dilligence and calling in favors."

Finding contact information is time consuming

"Guessing emails takes a long time and usually involves a lot of trial and error."

Want distilled, revlevent, personal information

"My biggest pain point is distilling information about the person...so I can come off smarter."

Want control over the prospecting process

"Outsourcing the prospecting process is time consuming and frustrating."

Different age groups require different contact methods

"You can't waste time with millennials. You have to tailor your communications to their behavior."

Initial research findings

Validated our strategic recommendation to add "nurturing" capabilities to Tier5's design, something high in demand for both key types of salespeople.

There are two very distinct types of salespeople with different requirements: targeted bulk leads or targeted individual search.

Users are willing to give feedback to improve Tier5's algorithm. This also has business implications as users may trial the product longer to see verify if data improves.

Harnassing social media and Klout scores are integral for users to better connect with growing millennial market.

The problem: redefined

We found that the saleperson's problem is two fold:

1 Quickly finding targeted leads with correct contact details

2 Difficult to find new and information to help nurture prospects

We Considered both User & Business Goals

Our solution: two high level experiences

Real-Time Connections

Targeted, instant, generation of rich, relevant leads and accurate contact information in bulk

Nurturing Connections

News and information to make effective connections and strong, first impressions

Design

We also provided them with daily updates and frequent Keynote presentations so they were always updated on our progress throughout the project. This strategy resulted in significant client buy-in and as a result a smooth running design process.
